The Problem

Digital tools promised efficiency and connection. Instead, we got friction and isolation.

Operational Friction

Universities and SMEs are overwhelmed by repetitive queries. Support staff are burnt out, and customers are frustrated.

Social Isolation

People are more “connected” online than ever, but feel more lonely. Social apps are designed for infinite scrolling, not genuine human connection.

Lack of Community Tools

Local businesses and groups want to build real-world communities, but lack simple, trusted tools to organize meetups and events.

A Two-Part Solution

An AI service that removes friction, and a community product that builds connection.

XOHOU AI (B2B Service)

Custom AI Agents for Universities & SMEs

  • Handles repetitive queries via voice & chat.
  • Frees up human staff for high-value tasks.
  • Integrates with existing workflows (Web, WhatsApp, IVR).
  • Our engine for funding and testing our AI backbone.

Neighborly (Community Product)

The Offline-First Social Network

  • Helps people meet neighbors and join micro-events.
  • Fights loneliness and screen addiction.
  • Platform for local businesses to host sponsored meetups.
  • Our vision for the future of social technology.
