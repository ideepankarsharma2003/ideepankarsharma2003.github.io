Navikarnam-2023

I'm thrilled to announce that we have won first place at Navikaranam 2023, the national level innovation fest held at National Institute of Technology, Uttarakhand! It's an incredible honor to have been recognized among so many talented individuals and teams from across the country. Our project "AI Enabled Baby Healthcare System using Reinforcement Learning", which is capable of automating and making lives of parents very convenient, was the result of months of hard work, dedication, and collaboration with my team members. I'm immensely proud of what we accomplished and couldn't be more grateful for the opportunity to showcase our innovation and creativity.

I'd like to take this opportunity to thank my team members Vipin Kumar, Himani Taneja, Gurpreet Kaur Jassal and Aditi Chauhan, without whom this achievement wouldn't have been possible. Their support and encouragement played a crucial role in our success. I'm looking forward to continuing to push the boundaries of innovation and entrepreneurship and contributing to India's thriving startup ecosystem. Thank you all for your support and encouragement along the way.

Not only was this an opportunity to showcase our innovation and creativity, but it was also a chance to meet and connect with new people. I'm grateful for the friendships and connections I made during the event and look forward to staying in touch with my fellow participants. I'd also like to express my sincere gratitude to the faculty and staff at NIT Uttarakhand for their support and guidance throughout our academic journey. Their mentorship and encouragement have been instrumental in helping us achieve this milestone.

Last but not least, I want to thank my teachers at Graphic Era Hill University for their unwavering support and belief in us. They've challenged us to grow and pushed us to reach new heights, and I couldn't be more grateful for their guidance.

I'm excited to continue my journey of innovation and entrepreneurship, and I'm grateful to have such a supportive community behind me.
